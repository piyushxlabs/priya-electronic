## Washing Machine Energy-Saving Tips

### 1. Wash with Full Loads

**Half load â‰  half electricity:**
- Actually uses 60-70% electricity of full load
- Wait for full load accumulation

**For Raiwala families:**
- Wash 2-3 times per week with full loads
- Better than daily small loads

### 2. Use Cold Water When Possible

**Hot water washing:**
- Uses 80-90% more electricity
- Heater element is biggest power consumer

**Cold water is fine for:**
- Regular dirty clothes
- Light stains
- Most Indian fabrics

**Use hot only for:**
- Heavy stains
- Sanitization needs
- Specific fabric care

### 3. Choose Right Wash Cycle

**Quick wash (15-30 min):**
- Lightly soiled clothes
- Uses 50% less energy

**Normal wash (45-60 min):**
- Regular soiling

**Heavy wash (90+ min):**
- Only when really needed

### 4. Spin Speed Optimization

**Higher RPM in spin cycle:**
- Removes more water
- Clothes dry faster
- Less energy needed for drying

**But for delicates:** Use lower RPM to prevent damage

### 5. Load Balancing

**Unbalanced load:**
- Machine vibrates excessively
- Works harder
- Wastes energy

**Proper loading:**
- Mix large and small items
- Distribute evenly
- Don't wrap clothes around agitator
