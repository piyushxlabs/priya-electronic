## Sign 5: Loud Noises, Vibrations, or Leaks

**Warning sounds:**
- **Grinding/Scraping:** Drum bearing failure (expensive repair)
- **Banging during spin:** Drum loose or damaged
- **Continuous dripping:** Pump or hose leak
- **Excessive vibration:** Foundation damage, needs replacement

**Why you shouldn't ignore:**
- Water damage to your floor (costly!)
- Electrical short circuit risk
- Machine can completely fail mid-cycle
- Repair often costs 60-70% of new machine
