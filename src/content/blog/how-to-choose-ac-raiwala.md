## Step 1: Calculate the Right Tonnage

**Tonnage** refers to cooling capacity. Too small won't cool properly; too large wastes electricity.

### Room Size Guide:
- **Up to 100 sq ft:** 0.75 ton
- **100-150 sq ft:** 1 ton
- **150-200 sq ft:** 1.5 ton
- **200-300 sq ft:** 2 ton
- **Above 300 sq ft:** 2.5 ton or split into multiple ACs

**Pro Tip from Priya Electronic:** In Raiwala, where summer heat can be intense, if your room has large windows facing west or is on the top floor, consider going up by 0.5 ton.

## Step 2: Inverter vs Non-Inverter

### Inverter AC (Recommended):
- **Energy savings:** 30-50% less electricity
- **Consistent cooling:** Adjusts compressor speed automatically
- **Better for Dehradun:** Handles temperature fluctuations well
- **Higher initial cost** but recovers in 2-3 years through savings

### Non-Inverter AC:
- **Lower purchase price**
- **Higher running costs**
- **On/off operation** (less efficient)
- **Good for:** Occasional use or small rooms

**Our Recommendation:** For Raiwala families using AC daily, inverter models are the smart choice.

- **5-Star:** Most efficient (lowest electricity bills)
